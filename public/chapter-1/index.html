Overview#

This chapter summarizes key ideas from Andrew Huberman’s discussion on building an optimal morning routine.

1. Natural Light Exposure#

Try to get sunlight within the first hour after waking.

2. Exercise#

Morning movement increases alertness.

3. Temperature#

Body temperature begins to rise after waking.

4. Cold Exposure#

Cold exposure can stimulate alertness.

Glossary#

Cortisol#

A hormone involved in metabolism and stress response.

Melatonin#

A hormone that regulates sleep cycles.
