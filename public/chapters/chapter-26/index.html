Chapter 26 — Coldest Winter

Coldest Winter from 808s & Heartbreak Album by Kanye West (one of my favorite track from 808)

“Coldest Winter”—an impassioned farewell to two important women in Kanye’s life—is his rendition of the 1983 song “Memories Fade” by Tears for Fears. The first of the women is his mother, Donda West, who died due to complications from cosmetic surgery in 2007. Donda had been a constant source of love and guidance in addition to taking on some of Kanye’s managerial responsibilities. The second is fashion designer Alexis Phifer with whom West had a six-year relationship (and two-year engagement). His mother’s death and Kanye’s demanding work schedule ultimately laid this romance to rest.
