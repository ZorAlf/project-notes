Prudent Self Care#

  • Practice Mindfulness: Pay attention to your thoughts and emotions without judgment. This allows you to identify unhealthy patterns and choose how you react.
  • Delay Gratification: Don’t be impulsive. Take time to consider the consequences of your actions before indulging in desires.
  • Set Boundaries: Learn to say no to protect your time and energy from things that don’t align with your values.

Practical Intelligence#

  • Seek Knowledge: Read, learn new things, and engage in critical thinking.
  • Consider Different Perspectives: Don’t jump to conclusions. Evaluate situations from all angles before making decisions.
  • Learn from Mistakes: Everyone makes them. See them as opportunities for growth and improvement.

Courage#

  • Face your Fears: Don’t avoid challenges, step outside your comfort zone and take calculated risks.
  • Focus on what can you control: Don’t worry too much about things that are beyond your control. Focus your energy on the things you can influence.
  • Develop Resilience: Life has setbacks. Learn to bounce back from difficulties and keep moving forward.

Justice#

  • Treat others with fairness and respect: Everyone deserves to be valued, regardless of background or beliefs.
  • Stand up for what’s rights: Speak out against injustice even if it’s uncomfortable.
  • Fulfill your responsibilities: Be honest, reliable, and keep your commitments.
