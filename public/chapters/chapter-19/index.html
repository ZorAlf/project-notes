Chapter 19 — Boredom Leads to Greatness

Boredom Leads to Greatness

Nowadays people avoid boredom when they go to the bathroom or while they eat, always bringing their phone. They are scared of boring things. Well, that is because boredom is uncomfortable and tough.

Boredom can lead us to greatness and give us ideas.

We are less bored than our ancestors were, but we are more afraid of boredom. Boredom is one of the best things that happen to you and something that changed the lives of many individuals, people are so afraid to be alone with our thoughts.

“By constantly trying to escape boredom, you will create the inability to stay alone with your thoughts. As a result, you will lose touch with yourself and what you want in life”

Your creativity will likely decrease to zero as you don’t give your mind to think. Escaping boredom will also be largely impactful to your focus, if yourself with activities that create large increases in dopamine and pleasure, everything else will seem boring and meaningless to you.

“Boredom is what leads to analysis and understanding yourself”

“It leads to your mind going to a creative state and coming up with new ideas you have never thought about before”

Nietzsche said:

“He who completely entrenches himself against boredom also entrenches himself again himself”

Immanuel Kant believes that boredom can inspire us to look beyond our immediate surroundings and explore new ideas and possibilities. By embracing boredom we can open up to new discoveries and innovation.

Use boredom to find your true self and practice mindfulness
