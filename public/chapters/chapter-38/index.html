Chapter 38 — Secret to Getting Better at Talking to People

Secret To Getting Better At Talking To People

How To Do It?

Be genuinely interested:

People love talking about themselves, so ask thoughtful questions and actively listen to their responses.

Listen actively:

Pay attention not just to their words but also their body language.

Open yourself up:

Share some things about yourself too. Conversations are a two-way street, and reciprocity helps build connections.

Nonverbal communication:

Maintain eye contact, smile, and use open body language.

Practice makes perfect:

Don’t be afraid to start conversations, even if they initially feel awkward. The more you practice, the more comfortable you’ll become.
