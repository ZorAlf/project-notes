Chapter 30 — The Less I Know The Better

This Chapter About Song

Currents - Tame Impala

The Less I Know The Better It is a psychedelic rock and disco-infused masterpiece that showcases the band’s knack for blending retro and contemporary sounds into an irresistibly catchy tune. Released as part of their critically acclaimed album “Currents” in 2015, the song became of Tame Impala’s most popular and commercially successful tracks.

At its core “The Less I Know The Better” is a tale of love and heartache, exploring themes of desire, jealousy, and longing. The lyrics tell the story of a protagonist who is infatuated with someone who is involved with another person, leading to feelings of frustration and confusion. Despite the emotional turmoil, the song maintains an upbeat and danceable rhythm, creating a juxtaposition between its melancholic lyrics and infectious groove.
