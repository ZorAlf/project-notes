## The Universe is Expanding

Since the Big Bang, the universe has been expanding at an incredible rate. Scientists estimate that the universe is expanding at a rate of 70 kilometers per second per megaparsec. This means that the distance between two galaxies that are one megaparsec (3.26 million light-years) apart will increase by 70 kilometers each second.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Expansion_of_the_universe

## Big Bang Theory

The Big Bang is a cosmological theory that explains the origin of the universe. The Universe was created from a large explosion about 13.8 billion years ago.

https://www.space.com/25126-big-bang-theory.html

## Black Holes

A black hole is a region of space with gravity so strong that even light cannot escape. Black holes form when massive stars collapse at the end of their lives.

https://science.nasa.gov/universe/black-holes/

## 2 Trillion Galaxies

The vast universe is made up of 2 trillion galaxies. Galaxies are collections of stars, dust, and dark matter bound together by gravity.
