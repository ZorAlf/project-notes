***"No Regrets, Move Forwards"***
— Jocko Willink

*"We often make mistakes"*

There are many ways to improve, and we all make mistakes. Often, the lessons we need to learn are right in front of us. The most important thing is to recognize that there is so much to learn from school, people, experiences, and life. However, you must process and absorb this information; you need to accept it and open your mind. This openness will allow you to learn and make progress.

Regret is worthless it does nothing for you, in fact, the only valuable thing in regret is the lesson you learn and the knowledge you gain, but walking around with regret gets you nothing, **so learn** and **move on**.

> *Don't let regret hold you back; don’t become its captive. Let it teach you and make you better. Use the fear of regret to inspire you to take action.*
