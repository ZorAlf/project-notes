***Life is a battle for me***

**Every single day.....**

**Im fighting with my own lust, thought, and temptation**


That's what I'm going through… no lie.

We are fighting against false impressions. We're fighting against destructive emotions like greed, fear, envy, and prejudice. We're fighting against our lower self to reach that one worthy of being cheered and celebrated. it's a fight that happens day in and day out. One that never really ends.

We'll never "win" but we can be great in our brief moment in the arena. We can be mighty as we fight back the beasts that are let out upon us.
