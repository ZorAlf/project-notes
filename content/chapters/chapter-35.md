**How geniuses got their ideas:**

 - Preparation & Deep Knowledge
 
Geniuses often spend years immersing themselves in their field. This deep understanding becomes a foundation for their ideas.

- Connecting the Dots

They're great at making unusual connections between seemingly unrelated concepts. It's like looking at puzzle pieces and seeing how they fit together in a new way.

- Persistence & experimentation

Great ideas rarely appear in a flash of lightning. Geniuses experiment, fail a lot, and refine their ideas over time.

*Can you become a genius? Yes. Here's how:*

**Cultivate Curiosity:**

Ask a question, explore different fields, and keep learning. The more you know, the more connections you can make.

**Embrace Experimentation:**

Don’t be afraid to try new things and fail. Failure is a stepping stone to success.

**Practice & Persistence:**

The more you create, the better you'll get at it. Creativity takes a practice.

**Seek Inspiration:**

Surround yourself with creative people and expose yourself to different ideas.
