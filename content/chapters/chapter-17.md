## Why We’re Addicted to Phones?

-  Phones are designed to capture and hold our attention using features like notifications, endless scrolling, and gamified elements.
- Dopamine plays a significant role in keeping us hooked by rewarding our brain for device interactions.

## The Impact of Phone Overuse

- Reduced focus and creativity.
- Increased anxiety and feelings of inadequacy, often due to social media.
- Disruption of real-life connections and experiences.

## Steps to Break the Addiction

-  **Awareness:** Understand your habits by tracking screen time and identifying triggers.
- **Boundaries:** Set rules for phone use, such as no-phone zones or  times.
- **Mindfulness:** Replace impulsive scrolling with purposeful actions, like reading or hobbies.

## Building a Healthier Relationship

- Use your phone as a tool, not a constant companion.
- Turn off unnecessary notifications.
- Prioritize face-to-face interactions and offline activities.
