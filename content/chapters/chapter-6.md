 1. **Practice Mindfulness**

 Regular meditation and mindfulness practices can help you clear your mind and focus better, leading to sharper thinking skills.
 
 2. **Stay Curious**

Cultivating a sense of curiosity about the world around you. Learn new things and seek out novel experiences. This will help to develop neural pathways that improve cognitive function.

 3. **Engage in Brain Exercises**

 Puzzles, word games, and memory games can all help to improve cognitive function and keep your mind sharp.

4. **Stay Active**

Regular exercise is beneficial for your brain as well as your body, physical activity helps to increase blood flow and oxygen to the brain, leading to better cognitive function.

5. **Get Enough Sleep**

Sleep is crucial to brain health and cognitive function, make sure to get enough restful sleep each night.

6. **Manage Stress** 

Chronic stress can have negative effects on brain function. Practice stress-reduction techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.

7. **Eat Healthy Diet**

The foods you eat can affect your brain function. Eating a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein can help to support the brain.
