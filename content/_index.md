### A Semi-Private Research Archive

---

Project Notes is a semi-private knowledge archive that grew from the habit of keeping small pocket notebooks.

Inspired by the video *[“Pocket Notebooks Can Save Your Mind – ParkNotes”](https://youtu.be/f_MddQW3U5E)*, this project is based on the idea that writing things down helps to keep thoughts alive and create an intellectual footprint.

What started as simple handwritten notes has slowly developed into a structured digital space. Here, I collect research notes, reflections, and ideas in a more organized way.

This project is both a hobby and a personal experiment, an effort to build a system for learning, thinking, and documenting knowledge over time.
